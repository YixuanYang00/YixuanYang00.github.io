About me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Zhejiang University. I am advised by Prof. Song Li. Prior to that, I received my B.S. degree in Pure and Applied Mathematics from Lanzhou University in 2022.
Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions or would like to discuss my research.

Research Interests
My research primarily focuses on mathematical foundations of Data Science, specifically in deep learning theory and non-smooth optimization. I am also interested in theory, algorithm and application of transformer and Large Language Model.
  • Data Science Problems: low-rank matrix recovery, phase retrieval and compressed sensing.
  • Deep Learning Theory: optimization, generalization and explanation of neural collapse phenomenon.
  • Optimization: Convergence and implicit bias of algorithms.
