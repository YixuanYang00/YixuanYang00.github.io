About me

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Zhejiang University, advised by Prof. Song Li. Prior to that, I received my B.S. degree in Pure and Applied Mathematics from Lanzhou University in 2022.

Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions or would like to discuss my research.

Research Interests

My research focuses on the mathematical foundations of data science, with an emphasis on deep learning theory and non-smooth optimization. I am also interested in the theory, algorithms, and applications of Transformers and large language models.

  • Data Science Problems: low-rank matrix recovery, phase retrieval, and compressed sensing.
  • Deep Learning Theory: optimization, generalization, and explanation of neural collapse phenomena.
  • Optimization: convergence and implicit bias of algorithms.
